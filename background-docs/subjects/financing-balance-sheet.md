| Assets | Liabilities |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Assets in Place** <br> * Existing Investments <br> * Generate cashflows today <br> * Includes long lived (fixed) and short-lived(working capital) assets | **Debt** <br> * Fixed Claim on cash flows <br> * Little or No role in management <br> * Fixed Maturity <br> * Tax Deductible |
| **Growth Assets** <br> * Expected Value that will be created by future investments | **Equity** <br> * Residual Claim on cash flows <br> * Significant Role in management <br> * Perpetual Lives |
